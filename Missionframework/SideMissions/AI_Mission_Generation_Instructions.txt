
    AI Instructions for Mission Generation (_4–_99):

    1. Mission Categories:
        - Disrupt
        - Supply
        - Intel

    2. Mission Difficulty:
        - Easy
        - Medium
        - Hard

    3. Mission Creation Process:
        - Templates should be created in the appropriate folder:
            e.g., 'SideMissions/disrupt/easy/', 'SideMissions/intel/medium/'
        - Templates should have unique IDs in their naming (e.g., MissionDisrupt_1, MissionIntel_2).

    4. Random Mission Generation:
        - AI should select one template at random from the selected category/difficulty folder.
        - _4–_99 missions are allowed, but system should use parameters to define their limits.

    5. Presets:
        - Use the following presets for enemy units, vehicles, and civilian NPCs:
            - opfor_preset
            - civ_preset
            - rebel_preset
            - blufor_preset
        - Presets should not be modified after mission creation (prevents mission corruption).
        - Vehicle classnames are derived from the respective preset categories, not hardcoded.

    6. Important Rules:
        - **Do not hardcode classnames or assets!**
        - Always check for the correct zone placement (missions should be placed randomly based on the zone available).
        - Always generate a variety of missions in terms of difficulty and category (easy missions should be lower difficulty, but scale appropriately).
